Connection and Synapse Counts
Connections
Connections between proofread cells are directed and thresholded with 5+ synapses.

Synapses
Synapse count refers to the number of synapses participating in connections as defined above. This is a small subset of all synapses in the FlyWire dataset, because most of them do not form a connection due to the 5+ synapse threshold per pair of cells. The total number of synapses in the FlyWire dataset is around 150 million.
