About FlyWire Connectome
Status
  • FlyWire Connectome is actively proofread and labeled - completeness and accuracy are improving daily
  • Expected public release date (along with Codex) is early 2023
Credits and Resources
  • FlyWire: A human-AI collaboration to map the fly connectome
  • To publish FlyWire data, please follow the instructions in this doc
  • FlyWire Terms of Service and Privacy notice
  • Supported by BRAIN Initiative grant MH117815 to Murthy and Seung
Data Sources
  • Cell segments were auto-generated from electron-microscopy images with AI. Primary contact: flywire@princeton.edu
  • Cell reconstructions were assembled from segments (proofread) by the FlyWire community (see FlyWire). Primary contact: flywire@princeton.edu
  • Synaptic connections were automatically detected using the Buhmann et al. method. Primary contact: flywire@princeton.edu
  • Free-form labels (cell identification tags) were provided by the FlyWire community. See the tagathon leaderboard and the specific list of authors in each cell info page for credits.
  • Hierarchical annotations (side, flow, super class, cell class, cell type, nerve and hemi-lineage) were assigned in collaboration with the Jefferis lab. Primary contact: Philipp Schlegel
  • Neurotransmitter types were predicted by Eckstein, Bates et al.. Primary contact: funkej@janelia.hhmi.org
  • Morphological similarity scores (NBLAST based) were computed for the central brain cells by Philipp Schlegel
