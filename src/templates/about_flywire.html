About FlyWire Connectome Data
Status
  • FlyWire Connectome is being actively proofread and labeled - completeness and accuracy are improving daily
  • Expected public release date (along with Codex) is Feb 2023
Credits and Resources
  • The FlyWire Project: A human-AI collaboration to map the fly connectome
  • To publish FlyWire data, please follow the instructions in this doc
  • FlyWire Terms of Service and Privacy notice
  • Supported by BRAIN Initiative grant MH117815 to Murthy and Seung
Data Sources
  • Cell segments: auto-generated from electron-microscopy images with AI
  • Synaptic connections: automatically detected using Buhmann et al. method
  • Cell reconstructions: assembled from segments (proofread) by the FlyWire community
  • Free-form cell identification labels: sourced by the FlyWire community
  • Cell classification (flow, super-class, class, sub-class, type): manually curated by Jefferis Lab
  • Neurotransmitter Types: predicted using AI, with selective overrides for known cell types
  • Morphological Similarity Scores (NBLAST based): computed for the central brain cells by Jefferis Lab
