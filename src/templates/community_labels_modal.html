Identification Labels
Community Identification Labels These are free-form textual labels/tags attached to FlyWire segments. Before indexing for search in Codex, multiple layers of filters and other types of transformations are applied to account for redundant / repetitive information, as well as corrections and other inaccuracies (the original log of labels for each cell is available for review in the cell detail page).

Cell identification labels were provided by the FlyWire community - hundreds of scientists around the world. See citation and credit guidelines on the FlyWire homepage.
